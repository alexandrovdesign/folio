Web Design, Identity, Development / 2025

An online store dedicated to Ukrainian vyshyvankas – traditional embroidery with a modern soul. The idea was to create a space that feels both cultural and current: where heritage meets design. The layout is calm and airy, letting the textures and details of each piece speak for themselves. It’s not just a store – it’s a quiet celebration of identity, craftsmanship, and the beauty of everyday tradition.
