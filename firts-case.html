Web Design, Branding, Development / 2025

An online pastry shop built to make ordering desserts as sweet as the desserts themselves. The idea was simple: let people easily customize cakes, place orders without the usual back-and-forth, and feel the brand’s warmth even through the screen. Everything – from the product cards to the checkout flow – was designed to feel effortless, cozy, and a little bit celebratory.

Role: Front-end Developer + Web Designer
